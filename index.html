Introduction

As an avid sports fan, I like to maintain an updated list of the top players in my two favorite sports leagues, the NBA and the NFL. It is a fun and challenging excercise to complete. It makes me stay up to date with how things are going in both leagues and gives me a good snapshot of how things are playing out in the current year. When it comes to these lists, there is a lot of different factors I take into account. The main of which are previous postseason peformance as well as current season performance. Then, there is some other stuff to consider like durability and personality. I hope you agree with my choices!
